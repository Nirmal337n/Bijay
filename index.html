About

Crafting stories on every plate

Based in Banepa, Kavre, Nepal, I am Chef Bijay Ghimire, blending contemporary techniques with timeless culinary traditions. From intimate local cuisines to grand dinner parties, every experience is designed to feel personal, refined, and unforgettable.

Trained in fine-dining kitchens and luxury hotels across Nepal, I have developed a deep passion for creating unforgettable dining experiences. My focus is on elevated Indian and international cuisine, refined plating, and seamlessly orchestrated dining journeys that tell a story.

With over a decade of experience in professional kitchens, I specialize in menu development, team leadership, and creating signature dishes that resonate with both traditionalists and modern food enthusiasts. Every dish is an opportunity to create memories.

Beyond professional culinary services, I am deeply committed to educating the next generation. I train aspiring students in Hotel Management, Professional Chef Skills, and Bakery & Pastry Arts, providing hands-on experience and industry-standard knowledge that prepares them for successful hospitality careers.
